  About Me

I’m currently a PhD candidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at UC San Diego, majoring in Intelligent Systems, Robotics, & Control, guided by Professor Mamadou Diagne. During the summer of 2024, I interned at Los Alamos National Laboratory, where I worked on estimation & control of gas flow in pipelines.

Before landing at UCSD, I earned my MS in Computer & Systems Engineering from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in 2022 and my BSc in Electrical & Electronic Engineering from the University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka back in 2017.
