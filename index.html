Biography

I am an expert in control systems with a focus on robotics and intelligent systems. Currently, I am pursuing my PhD at the University of California, San Diego. My research involves control theory, optimization, and data-driven control strategies for advanced robotic applications.

Research Interests

My work aims to advance the reliability, efficiency, and versatility of autonomous robotic systems. I focus on safe control, data-driven control, event-triggered systems, and robotics applications. My contributions include the development of optimization methods for large-scale control systems, control theory for nonlinear systems, and reinforcement learning applications.
